Dungeonmaster's Helper

Overview

Dungeonmaster's Helper is a digitally created character sheet that generates a character sheet for Dungeons and Dragons players. The program helps players unfamiliar with character creation by prompting them for various inputs, and also includes digital dice in order to automatically update the character sheet with changes in level, stats, health, etc.

Personas

Name Details Goals
Michelle, the Dungeonmaster A 21 year old student, youngest sister, and acting dungeonmaster for the family D&D games. Wants a better way to keep all of the character sheets organized, and make sure her brothers can't cheat by erasing or changing their stats or HP when no one is looking, and "accidentally" rolling the dice off the table.
John, the Experienced Player A 31 year old IT professional who plays with his family in his spare time. Doesn't like having to keep track of his stats as he levels up, or doing lots of math in the margins of his character sheet to track damage. Has limited free time to play, and wants to make the most of it.
Chris, the Newbie A 27 year old middle sibling who enjoys causing mischeif. Chaotic Evil. New to the game, and not familiar with how to fill out a character sheet or track stats. As a casual player only looking to play with family, wants an easy way to play without taking time to learn all of the particulars.

Problem Scenarios

Problem Scenario Current Alternatives Value Proposition
Michelle the Dungeonmaster wants to make sure her brothers can't cheat on their character sheets. Michelle tries to watch carefully when they write in their stats, damage, HP, and other game info. Creating a digital character sheet that she could modify, or that would be updated automatically by digital dice rolls would help avoid cheating, and also likely help the game to move along more quickly.
John, the Experienced Player wants all the math to be done for him automatically every time he rolls. John uses a calculator to keep track of his HP and damage. A digital character sheet with automatic updates for all of his moves would mean John doesn't have to update anything manually, so he is free to focus on how to get the basilisk to go after his brother instead of him.
Chris, the Newbie needs an easy way to fill and update his character sheet because he is not familiar with the game. There are some digital character sheets online but they won't update based on his gameplay. Creating a tool to prompt new players for the inputs that need to be added to their character sheet makes the game more accessible. Adding the option to have the sheet updated to reflect gameplay is useful to new players as well.

User Stories

As Michelle the Dungeonmaster, I want to be able to uphold the integrity of our games by digitalizing the character sheets, and the dice rolling in order to avoid players modifying their character sheets and stats after their creation, or fudging the numbers of a bad roll.
As John the Experience Player, I want to be able to use one program to keep track of my character stats that responds to my digital dice rolls. I want my character sheet to immediately respond to new information.
As Christopher the Newbie, I don't want to have to read a bunch of player manuals before I can play this game with my family. I want to be prompted for all the necessary information, and have my character sheet created for me automatically based on what I put in.
As Christopher the Newbie, I also don't want to have to learn the math behind different types of events, and would prefer if it could be calculated for me, and updated in the appropriate spot on my character sheet.
